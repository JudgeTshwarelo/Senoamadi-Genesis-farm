About Us

Background

At Senoamadi Poultry Industries, we aim to provide farm-fresh eggs, chicken meat, and organic poultry feed to our customers. Our mission is to deliver healthy, organic, and affordable poultry products.

VISION:

1. To provide affordable, healthy, and high-quality poultry products in South Africa.

2. To help fight poverty nationwide by creating jobs in the community.

MISSION:

To provide communities with fresh, affordable, and high-quality poultry products while promoting food security and creating local jobs.

VALUES:

  • Integrity
  • Quality
  • Sustainability
  • Community Impact

Goals and Objectives:

Senoamadi Poultry’s objectives aim to facilitate economic growth and create jobs within local communities. Our strategic focus areas include:

  • Growing the economy
  • Job creation and improving community livelihood
  • Economic empowerment
  • Poverty reduction
  • Improving health and social status
  • Reducing crime and corruption
  • Promoting safe and sustainable environments

Achieving these objectives requires strategic partnerships, mentorship, and collaboration with investors. With shared commitment, we can push back the frontiers of poverty and uplift our communities.
